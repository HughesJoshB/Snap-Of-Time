Weddings

With wedding photography, its not just the major moments that need to be documented but also those small intement moments. A professional wedding photographer not only has the high end equipment and practiced talent to capture and edit photos from your wedding; they also understand that these moments are both natural and created. We will manage your posed shots and direct you and your guest to get the best pictures. We have a keen eye for the candid moments throughout the ceremony and reception. All of your pictures will be edited and delivered to you with full copyright permissions, so you and your family can enjoy your gallery in its entirety without being forced to spend more money after the wedding.

Scenic
